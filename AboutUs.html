About Us

GAP Hardware was founded in 1964 by brothers James and Edwin Moore whose core business was the supply and installation of household flooring. We currently employ more than 50 staff throughout our warehouse and distribution centre. Since then, the business has expanded into the commercial sector. We now supply hardware products to over 180 clients throughout the Greater Canberra and Queanbeyan regions.
